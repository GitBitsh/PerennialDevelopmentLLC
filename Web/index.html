ABOUT

Perennial Development LLC is a forward-thinking real estate development company specializing in creating sustainable, innovative, and community-focused residential and commercial properties. Our name 'Perennial' reflects our commitment to developing properties that stand the test of time, much like perennial plants that bloom year after year.

Perennial Development, LLC was founded in 2019 through a partnership between Britt Soderberg, a Hudson, NY based Real Estate Entrepreneur and a privately owned Opportunity Zoned Fund.

The company is currently focusing on developing properties in Opportunity Zones in the Hudson Valley. Emphasizing a forward-looking approach, it's aiming to construct unique, micro-fill buildings within the five boroughs of Manhattan, NY.

Hudson, NY, located approximately 100 miles north of Manhattan, NY was chosen as an Opportunity Zone offered through the Tax Cuts and Job Acts of 2017, qualifying it for financial benefits to investors in order to stimulate the city's economy.

Perennial Development, LLC was able to leverage previously owned real estate located within the city and utilized Hudson's Opportunity Zone status to obtain financial backing from a privately held Opportunity Zone Fund to form the company and work toward the completion of its first project.
